Our Story

GharSeMeal connects students to home chefs in their neighbourhood. We believe everyone deserves a meal made with love, regardless of where they live.

Founded by ex-hostellers who were tired of the same oily mess in mess halls, we realized the solution was living right next door—passionate home cooks who wanted to share their culinary heritage and earn.

"Born from a real problem — bad PG food and an amazing aunty next door."

Why GharSeMeal?

🍲

Homemade Quality

No mass production. Every meal is cooked in a real home kitchen using fresh, hand-picked ingredients and traditional recipes.
